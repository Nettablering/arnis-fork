# Landing Copy — Worldbuilders Alpha Waitlist

For the homepage waitlist email-capture form and the `/alpha` page CTA. Tone matches
the Aurora Atlas voice: cartographic, plain-spoken, low-hype. International by default.

---

## Primary headline (hero, /alpha page)

> Help us bake the world before everyone else gets a tile.

## Sub-headline

> Worldbuilders turns your hometown into a Roblox idle empire built on OpenStreetMap.
> We are opening 50 hand-picked alpha seats across the world, then 500, then 5 000.
> Apply once — we'll route you to the right stage when it opens.

## Three-line "what you get" block

- **Stage 1 (Alpha · 50 seats)** — bi-weekly call with the founder, private Discord,
  every bug you find gets named after you, plus the permanent in-game Founder badge.
- **Stage 2 (Closed beta · 500 seats)** — invite-code access, weekly State-of-Beta,
  in-game `/feedback` direct to Rolf's screen.
- **Stage 3 (Soft launch · 5 000 seats)** — early access in PH, NZ and NO before
  the global pin drops. Clips encouraged.

## CTA copy (button text)

- Primary CTA: **Apply for an alpha seat**
- Secondary CTA: **Just put me on the waitlist**

## Email-capture form (homepage variant — short)

> Drop a pin on the alpha. We'll email when a seat opens for your region and device.
>
> [ email ]   [ Drop pin ]
>
> By signing up you agree to occasional pre-launch emails. No spam, no resale. Unsubscribe in one click.

## Long-form applicant intro (above the form on /alpha)

> Worldbuilders is a real-world idle game: claim the actual buildings on your street,
> upgrade them, raid your neighbours. It runs on a Rust pipeline that bakes
> OpenStreetMap into Roblox tiles.
>
> The alpha is small on purpose. We want testers who will actually load the game
> three times a week, click the rough edges, and tell us what hurts. In return you
> get a direct line to the studio, a Founder badge that never appears in any later
> cosmetic drop, and a permanent listing in the credits.
>
> This is a global programme. You do not have to be in Norway. We do need a parent
> or guardian co-signing if you're under 18 — Roblox COPPA rules, not ours.

## Confirmation copy (after submit)

> Application received. We read every one. You'll hear from us within 14 days with
> either an invite code or a "not this wave — try the next one" note. Either way you
> stay on the list.

## Rejection copy (used by operator runbook)

> Thank you for applying to the Worldbuilders alpha. We didn't have a seat that fit
> this wave — we usually need to balance devices, regions and timezones, and a wave
> can fill before a great applicant gets through. You're still on the waitlist for
> the next stage and we'll email when it opens.

  OpenStreetMap-powered Roblox idle empire. Global programme; iPhone, Android, iPad
